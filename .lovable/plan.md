
# TimeTravel Agency — Interactive Webapp

## 1. Landing Page (Hero Section)
- Elegant light design with sophisticated typography and subtle warm accents
- Full-width hero section with a captivating headline and animated background (parallax or subtle motion)
- Brief agency pitch: "Voyagez à travers le temps avec élégance"
- CTA button scrolling to the destinations gallery
- Smooth fade-in animations on load

## 2. Header & Navigation
- Sticky navigation bar: Logo, Destinations, À propos, Contact
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu

## 3. Destinations Gallery (3 Interactive Cards)
- **Paris 1889** — Belle Époque, Tour Eiffel, Exposition Universelle
- **Crétacé -65 millions d'années** — Dinosaures, nature préhistorique
- **Florence 1504** — Renaissance, Michel-Ange, art et architecture
- Each card features your uploaded images, hover effects, and an expandable detail view with description, highlights, and fictional pricing
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations

## 4. AI Chatbot Widget
- Floating chat bubble (bottom-right corner)
- Opens a chat window with the TimeTravel Agency personality
- Powered by Lovable AI (requires enabling Lovable Cloud)
- Answers questions about destinations, pricing, and travel recommendations
- Professional yet warm tone, passionate about history

## 5. Destination Quiz (Personalized Recommendation)
- Interactive 4-question quiz to recommend the ideal destination
- Questions about experience type, preferred era, environment, and activities
- AI-generated personalized recommendation at the end

## 6. Footer
- Agency info, social links, credits
- "Projet pédagogique — M1/M2 Digital & IA" mention

## Design Direction
- Light, elegant theme with refined typography
- Subtle gold or warm accent colors for luxury feel
- Smooth scroll animations (fade-in, scale) using Tailwind CSS animations
- Mobile-first responsive design
